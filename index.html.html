Who I Am

Engineer. Researcher.
Changemaker.

I am a PhD researcher in Chemical Engineering & Applied Chemistry at the University of Toronto, specialising in sustainability integration within AI-enabled materials discovery systems for renewable energy. My work sits at the intersection of advanced engineering, environmental science, and strategic business thinking — shaped by over a decade of hands-on experience in manufacturing, supply chain, and project leadership across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and the United Kingdom.

From leading 100+ person production teams at PZ Cussons to delivering healthcare data intelligence at NHS England, I bring a rare blend of technical rigour and real-world impact to every challenge I take on. My research on Bio-Resins and net-zero carbon transitions reflects my core belief: that sustainable engineering is not just an academic pursuit — it is a business imperative.
